Benefits of the Map


Increased Awareness: This platform brings visibility to areas that may be experiencing harmful or unlawful ICE encounters, allowing individuals to report incidents and highlight locations where civil rights violations may be occurring. By tracking these incidents, the map also raises awareness of areas that are at high risk for future ICE actions, helping to inform communities and authorities.

Real-Time Data and Monitoring: It helps ensure that both community members and advocacy groups can stay informed, respond swiftly, and monitor areas of concern as ICE operations unfold, which is crucial for both protection and intervention.


  • Community Empowerment: By contributing to the map, people can help shape a collective response to ICE activities, raising awareness of their rights and offering a platform for solidarity.
  • Targeted Resource Allocation: With accurate, real-time data on where ICE encounters are most frequent, organizations, community groups, and local governments can allocate resources more effectively.
  • Accountability and Risk Mitigation: By tracking ICE encounters, the map holds ICE and local law enforcement accountable for their actions.
